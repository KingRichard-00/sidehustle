A TRIBUTE TO ZAC POONEN

Zac Poonen is a well known preacher of the gospel based in india whose impact has been felt globally. He was born in November 5, 1939 and has married to Annie Poonen since 1968. He has been in service to the Lord for over 50years. His sermons which are freely available on the net has been a blessing to millions of souls round the globe including myself. He is a man who is offered completely to the Lord. He performs responsibility over many churches in india and overseas.
He is a devout and a faithful teacher of the Word. The books written by him count up to 25 and in addition to that he has also written numerous christian articles in english which have been translated into foreign languages. He caters for himself and his fanily through the profession of making tents and does not receive any form/kind of salary for his service to the Lord. Neither does he receive any payment for his books, articles, CDs or DVDs that are publshed by Fellowship Cebtre, Bangalore. His life is worthy of emulation and he sets a footprints for the younger generation of ministers who wants to serve the Lord whole heartedly.
